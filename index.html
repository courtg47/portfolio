About Me

Hi!

Welcome to my site. My name is Courtney and I'm a self-taught Front End Web Developer. I really enjoy JavaScript and seeing a website come to life through it but I also love CSS and making the design and user interface something a little more special.

My professional background is quite eclectic and I've worn many hats so far: U.S. Marine, combat veteran, Tier 1 Help Desk Associate, Intelligence Analyst, Personal Trainer, published blogger, and Front End Web Developer.

My experience with web development goes back to 2009 when I created my first website for my University rugby club. I didn't stop there. I continued building websites for a business I owned, a personal blog, then a fitness blog. I indulged in WordPress and other skills including SEO, internet marketing, content marketing, and other skills along the way. Since then, I began learning all I could about coding and web development and in the process, I learned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, among other technologies. I then decided to move my full-time career to Front End Web Development entirely.

Feel free to peruse my portfolio below and check out my work. Many projects listed are completed projects from the Free Code Camp Front End Development Certificate. More will follow. This website was also created from scratch by me utilizing HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, and made fully responsive with media queries. This portfolio website is self-hosted on Host Gator, and I am utilizing WordPress to supply the backend. Feel free to check out the source code for this site on Github (link).

And don't forget to contact me once you are finished! Looking forward to hearing from you soon.
